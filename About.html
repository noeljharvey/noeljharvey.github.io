Sadie Horler

Accompanying her guitar with effortless, crystal clear and captivating vocals, Sadie engages her audience with warmth and authenticity.


Her songs are written with heartfelt lyrics inspired by events and people around her. Although Sadie has only been performing in front of live audiences for a relatively short period of time, she has certainly got people talking!
Recently Sadie has attracted many more followers of her music from around the world after her successful appearance on Balcony TV .

Over the last year, the BBC introducing team in Devon have played 7 of Sadie's tracks on their show, with the first song she wrote and sent in being played as upload of the week. They have been massively supportive of Sadie's music and have described her as 'phenomenal' and 'One of the finest young female artists they have discovered, in 2015'
